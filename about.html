About us

Two people who decided to build something real.

Riko Digital is a small agency that does focused, quality work. No fluff. No bloated teams. Just two people who know their stuff and care about the results they deliver.

Most people our age are waiting for the right moment to start. We decided to stop waiting.

Riko Digital started because we saw a real gap: small businesses paying big-agency prices for generic work delivered by people they never actually speak to. We knew we could do better — and we had the skills to prove it.

We're two high school juniors at Westford Academy who've already shipped real projects, built our own machines, and spent more time learning than most people twice our age. We take this seriously. Our clients do too.

When you work with Riko Digital, you're working directly with the people building your website, writing your emails, and setting up your automations. That's not a pitch — it's just how we operate.
