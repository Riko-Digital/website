What we offer

Services built for small businesses that want to grow.

Everything we do is focused on one thing: making your business more visible, more efficient, and more valuable to the people you serve.

Service 01

Website Design & Build

Your website is the first thing most potential customers see. It needs to be fast, clean, and built with a clear purpose — turning visitors into leads. We design and build custom sites from scratch, tailored to your brand and your goals.

No page builders. No bloated templates. Just a site that's built properly, loads quickly on any device, and actually represents your business well. We walk you through the whole process and hand it off with a full walkthrough so you know exactly what you have.

  • Custom design — no templates
  • Mobile-responsive across all screen sizes
  • Performance and speed optimization
  • Basic on-page SEO setup
  • Contact forms and conversion elements
  • Launch support and owner walkthrough
Service 02

Website Maintenance

Websites don't run themselves. Plugins go out of date, security vulnerabilities pop up, and things break. Most business owners don't have the time or technical background to stay on top of it — and they shouldn't have to.

We handle ongoing maintenance so your site stays fast, secure, and up to date. Need to update your hours, swap out a photo, or add a new page? Send us a message and it's handled. No tickets, no waiting on a support queue.

  • Regular software, theme, and plugin updates
  • Security monitoring and vulnerability patching
  • Content updates on request
  • Uptime monitoring and issue response
  • Monthly performance check
Service 03

Social Media Management

Being consistent on social media matters — but creating content every week is time-consuming when you're running a business. We take that off your plate entirely. From strategy and content creation to scheduling and posting, we keep your channels active and on-brand.

We write the copy, create the visuals, and build a posting cadence that makes sense for your audience. You stay visible without spending your evenings figuring out what to post.

  • Content strategy and monthly planning
  • Post copy and graphic creation
  • Scheduling and publishing across platforms
  • Basic engagement monitoring
  • Monthly performance recap
Service 04

Email Marketing

Email is one of the highest-return marketing channels available — if it's done right. A well-written email to a warm list will outperform most paid ads. We set up and manage your email marketing from strategy to send, including automated sequences that work for you around the clock.

Whether you want to send a regular newsletter, announce a promotion, or set up a welcome sequence for new customers, we handle the writing, design, and logistics.

  • Campaign strategy and content calendar
  • Email copywriting and design
  • Automated welcome and follow-up sequences
  • List management and basic segmentation
  • Open rate, click, and performance tracking
Service 05

AI & Automation

There are tasks in your business that happen over and over — answering the same customer questions, sending follow-up emails, moving data between tools. With the right setup, those tasks can run automatically. We identify the opportunities, build the workflows, and deploy them so you can stop doing things manually.

From customer-facing AI chatbots to back-end integrations between your tools, we use the latest AI and automation platforms to help your business run more efficiently — without hiring more staff.

  • Business workflow analysis and opportunity mapping
  • AI chatbot setup, training, and deployment
  • Zapier / Make automation builds
  • Third-party tool integration and configuration
  • Ongoing monitoring and optimization
